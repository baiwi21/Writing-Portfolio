# Troubleshooting Guide

## Cannot Log In

**Possible causes:**

* Incorrect password
* Account not registered

**Solution:**

* Reset your password
* Check your email and try again

---

## Tasks Not Saving

**Possible causes:**

* Internet connection issues

**Solution:**

* Refresh the page
* Check your connection

---

## Application Not Loading

**Solution:**

* Clear browser cache
* Try another browser
